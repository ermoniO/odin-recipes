Ramen

Desription

This is one of the most delicious recipes of Ramen you will have laid eyes on. Ramen is a delight in the japanese culture and a something I thoroughly enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Sesame oil
  • Firm Tofu
  • Vegetable broth
  • Shitake Mushrooms
  • Ramen noodles
  • Miso paste
  • bok choy

Instructions

  1. Heat sesame oil in your pan and fry onion, garlic and ginger. Saute until browned.
  2. Boil ramen in a separate pot of water
  3. Add vegetable broth vegetables, mushrooms, miso paste over the frying pan and add enough water to make it into a soup.
