Sweet Corn on the Cob

Who doesnt enjoy the sweet buttery corn as a side dish to their favorite comfort food? With this simple recipe your sure to overindulge in this dish!

Ingredients

  • Corn on the Cob
  • Butter
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Pinch of Rosemary

Steps

  1. Put the corn in a pot and cover with butter.
  2. Sprinkle a pinch of rosemary and salt on the corn and mix around
  3. Let cook for 10 minutes or until browned.
  4. Plate it and enjoy!
