Homemade Lemonade

Lemonade in the grocery store at at your favorite restaurant may be nice, but comes with a price tag! Why not make you own lemonade that you can enjoy anytime for the fraction of the cost!

Ingredients

  • The juice of 5 lemons
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1.5 cups of sugar

Steps

  1. Add the juice from the lemons and room temperature water together.
  2. Stir in the sugar until it is completely dissolved.
  3. Add ice and enjoy!
